A Summary Who Am I?

I was born and raised in Arizona, I spent most of my early years near Phoenix until I moved to a small farming town in northern Arizona. Shortly after graduating highschool, I served a 24 month service mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints in Resistencia, Argentina. Upon returning home, I decided to continue my education at Brigham Young University where I am a senior in their finance program. One of my biggest passions in life, and what I want to dedicate myself to professionally, is the healthcare industry. While in Argentina, I gained a more profound understanding of the importance affordable, accessible, and effective health care. In the future, I hope to be able to improve these healthcare topics not only in the United States but also abroad. When I am not working or in school I enjoy the occasional video game, fishing adventure, or personal finance podcast.

Important Qualities

I am a passionate individual and often find myself obsessing over topics I find interesting. Some of these obsessions include good food, beautiful scenery, professional sports, thoughtful acts of kindness, and more. Despite my overzealousness, I am not one to make hasty or rash decisions. I enjoy approaching a decision in a structured and methodical format so that I can move forward with confidence that I have made the best decision. Similarly, I love executing these decisions, whether it be a planned vacation, tasty recipe, or business plan I love the journey of completing something start to finish.
