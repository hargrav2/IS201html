The Power of Investing While Young

  1. Longer Growth Horizon
    • Stock Market has historically grown 7-10% YoY, making each year an investor is in the market an opportunity to grow the value of their portfolio
    • The longer an investor is in the market the less susceptible their portfolio becomes to recessio87n and economic downturn
  • Compound Interest and Reinvested Dividends
    • Compound interest allows your investments to have additional growth from their past growth which makes investing while young the perfect opportunity to snowball your portfolio in the future
    • Reinvesting Dividends synergizes with compound interesting, increasing the velocity at which your portfolio gains value

  • Maximize the Power of Tax Deferred Accounts
    • Investment accounts such as Roth IRA allow investor to allocate funds with current taxable income so that in the future they can withdraw their investments without paying capital gains taxes
    • Unfortunately, these accounts have restrictions that limit the amount of money investors can contribute each year. This makes starting an investing journey early even more lucrative because it enables the investor to shelter more future income from taxes
